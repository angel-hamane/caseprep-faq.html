Probate Case Prep – Frequently Asked Questions

This course helps you organize facts, evidence, and strategy inputs so you’re ready for court or a lawyer consultation. It is not petition drafting or legal advice.

About the Course

What is Probate Case Prep?
It’s a pre-petition prep course: you’ll learn to understand the process, evaluate valid legal grounds, and assemble a clean case binder (timeline, documents, witness notes). It does not teach petition writing — that’s covered in the flagship Petition course.

Who is this course for?
For people considering probate litigation in California who want to clarify if they have a case and organize evidence before meeting an attorney or filing pro per.

Scope & Limitations

Does this course guarantee I have a case?
No. This course is educational only. It helps you prepare and think critically, but only the court can decide standing, validity, and outcome.

Will this course save me money?
Yes, often. Attorneys charge $350–$500/hour to sort through your documents. By coming prepared, you save them time — which saves you money — and you’re in control of your case story.

Will this course show me how to draft petitions?
No. That’s covered in the flagship Petition course.

Technical & Logistics

Do you provide tech support?
No. I can’t fix your computer, printer, or internet connection. Please contact your device provider or a local service for tech issues.

What materials will I need?
A computer, internet access, and a binder or folder system (physical or digital) to organize documents, evidence, and notes.

Legal & Education Clarification

Is this legal advice?
No. I am not a lawyer, and this course does not provide legal advice. It’s purely educational.

What if I need legal advice?
Please consult a qualified probate attorney for guidance about your specific situation.
